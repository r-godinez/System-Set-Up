=== MANUAL SETTINGS REQUIRED ===
The following settings need to be changed manually:

1. ACCENT COLOR:
   - Right-click on desktop > Personalize
   - OR: Settings > Personalization > Colors
   - Select 'Metal Blue' or your preferred accent color
   - Toggle 'Show accent color on Start and taskbar' ON

2. DISABLE WIDGETS:
   - Right-click on taskbar > Taskbar settings
   - OR: Settings > Personalization > Taskbar
   - Turn OFF 'Widgets' toggle

3. VERIFY OTHER SETTINGS:
   - Check that Dark mode is enabled
   - Verify taskbar is centered
   - Confirm search box is hidden

If registry changes don't apply automatically:
1. Restart Windows Explorer (Ctrl+Shift+Esc > Windows Explorer > Restart)
2. Or reboot your computer
3. Or manually change in Settings > Personalization
